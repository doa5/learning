Manhattan Cafe, (Japanese:マンハッタンカフェ, 5 March 1998 - August 2015) was a Japanese Thoroughbred racehorse and sire. Unraced as a juvenile, he began his racing career as a three-year-old in 2001. He improved throughout the season, winning three minor races before developing into a top class stayer in autumn when he recorded Grade I wins in the Kikuka Sho and the Arima Kinen. He won the Tenno Sho as a four-year-old.
